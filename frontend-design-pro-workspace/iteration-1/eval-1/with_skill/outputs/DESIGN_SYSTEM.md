# Design System: Nexus Private Banking

## 1. Concept: "The Digital Vault"
The design aims to evoke absolute security, exclusivity, and technological precision. It uses a "Luxury Tech" aesthetic that blends Swiss minimalism with modern interactive elements.

## 2. Visual Language
- **Grid**: Strict 12-column grid with generous negative space to allow the content to "breathe" and feel premium.
- **Glassmorphism**: Subtle use of background blurs (`backdrop-filter`) and thin borders (`border-white/10`) to create depth without clutter.
- **Micro-interactions**: Subtle hover states, smooth scroll reveals, and floating animations for high-value assets (like the credit card mockup).

## 3. Color Palette
- **Primary (Midnight)**: `#020617` - Deep, stable base.
- **Accent (Mint)**: `#34D399` - Fresh, energetic, and associated with growth/wealth.
- **Text (Slate)**: `#94a3b8` - Highly legible, professional secondary color.
- **Gradients**: White-to-Slate for headings to create a metallic/premium feel.

## 4. Typography
- **Headings**: `Plus Jakarta Sans` - A geometric sans-serif that feels modern and approachable yet authoritative.
- **Body**: `Inter` - The industry standard for digital legibility and trust.

## 5. Iconography
- **Library**: Lucide Icons
- **Style**: 1.5px stroke weight. Thin, elegant lines that match the precision of the overall design.
